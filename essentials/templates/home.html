What is Boost?

Boost is a streamlined open platform for creators and sponsors to connect.

Connect Effortlessly

Gone are the times of cold calls and agencies. Boost allows you to connect with little effort and great reward.

Find Your Match

With our open marketplace, finding the perfect match has never been easier.

Escrow

We want both parties to be happy with the transaction, so Boost works with Escrow.com to utilize escrow transaction, this way both parties can be sure that the deal is exactly how they want.

Streamlined Process

Our streamlined process guides you through your transaction in a matter of minutes.
