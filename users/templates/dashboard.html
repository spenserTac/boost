Dashboard Help
Creators Orders
ONLY For Creators
Overview


This is where all orders for your creator listings will be. When a sponsors views and orders your listing in the open creator marketplace, specifications for their order will appear here.

Options

From here you can accept the order, and the order will then be placed in Accepted Sponsor Orders (more on that below), or you can decline the order.


Sponsor Orders
ONLY For Sponsors
Overview


This is where all orders for your sponsor listings will be. When a creator views and orders your listing in the open sponsor marketplace, specifications for their order will appear here.

Options

From here you can accept the order, and you'll then give the creator some details on what you'd like, or you can decline the order.


Accepted Sponsor Orders
ONLY For Creators
Overview


This is where all the orders that you've accepted will appear. When you send you content over for the sponsor to review, they can add some edits to the content (which you'll be able to see).

Options

From here you can send the content you've created for the sponsor to review (PDF file), or you can withdraw from the order.


Creators To Review
ONLY For Sponsors
Overview


This is where you'll review the creators content that they've sent over. When a creator has accepted your order, they'll create what you've orderd, and then they'll send it over for your review. You'll be able to view the PDF file here.

Options

From here you can approve of the order (then you'll be sent to escrow.com to complete the process.), or you can add some edits to the creators content (which they'll be able to see), and then the process will happen all over again.


Order History
For BOTH Creators and Sponsors
Overview


This is where you'll be able to view all current and past orders.

Options

From here you can view current and past orders that you've been a part of. You'll be able to see all info on them in case you'd like to make another order with that creator/sponsor.

Dashboard Help
Creators Orders
ONLY For Creators
Overview


This is where all orders for your creator listings will be. When a sponsors views and orders your listing in the open creator marketplace, specifications for their order will appear here.

Options

From here you can accept the order, and the order will then be placed in Accepted Sponsor Orders (more on that below), or you can decline the order.


Sponsor Orders
ONLY For Sponsors
Overview


This is where all orders for your sponsor listings will be. When a creator views and orders your listing in the open sponsor marketplace, specifications for their order will appear here.

Options

From here you can accept the order, and you'll then give the creator some details on what you'd like, or you can decline the order.


Accepted Sponsor Orders
ONLY For Creators
Overview


This is where all the orders that you've accepted will appear. When you send you content over for the sponsor to review, they can add some edits to the content (which you'll be able to see).

Options

From here you can send the content you've created for the sponsor to review (PDF file), or you can withdraw from the order.


Creators To Review
ONLY For Sponsors
Overview


This is where you'll review the creators content that they've sent over. When a creator has accepted your order, they'll create what you've orderd, and then they'll send it over for your review. You'll be able to view the PDF file here.

Options

From here you can approve of the order (then you'll be sent to escrow.com to complete the process.), or you can add some edits to the creators content (which they'll be able to see), and then the process will happen all over again.


Order History
For BOTH Creators and Sponsors
Overview


This is where you'll be able to view all current and past orders.

Options

From here you can view current and past orders that you've been a part of. You'll be able to see all info on them in case you'd like to make another order with that creator/sponsor.
