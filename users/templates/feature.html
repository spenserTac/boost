For Creator

This platform is supposed to connect creators and sponsors, through an open and free marketplace, designed to skip the tedious and time consuming task of trying to find sponsorship or quality content to sponsor. As of right now, only blogs are supported, but in the future, this platform will support every type of creator (YouTubers, Instagramers, etc.).

NOTE: If you initate an order (you contact the sponsor), when the order is finalized, Boost will take a service fee of 10%. If the sponsor initates the order, you'll recive the full payemnt (the sponsor pays a 10% service fee.)

Share your feedback on concepts and features of the Boost platform. I'm looking for feedback on usability, concepts, flow-process, etc.

Please answer all five of these questions and feel free to provide extra feedback if you want, I'd really appreciate it.
  • 1. Is this platform somthing you would use? Why or why not?

  • 2. Is the presentation/layout of this webiste confusing in any way? If so, how?

  • 3. Would you prefer using this platform for finding sponsorship over the traditional route (agency or cold calling/emailing)? Why?

  • 4. Is the process of an order (accept/decline -> send content for sponsor to review -> edits or approved) seem tedeous or do you like it for the assurance that the sponsor will approve of the content you made. Explain.

  • 5. Do you like that the order is finalized through an escrow? Why?
Features Under Construction (no feedback needed):
  • 1. creators sending in files for review (files are not viewable).

  • 2. Filter system is not complete.

  • 3. Escrow/payemnt process is not functional.

  • 4. Creators Google Analytics is not functional.

  • 5. Lisitng pictures are not working (they're not customizable).

  • 6. Account Page is not fully functional or doesn't look good.

  • 7. There are no alerts when somthing happens (order is made, order is accepted, etc.)

  • 8. None of the privacy policy, terms of service, etc. links work.
